Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 4, 2024

Thank you for using Compressr. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection, use, and protection practices.

1. Information We Collect

Compressr respects user privacy and collects minimal data:

  • We collect no personal data - The app works completely locally
  • Your video files stay on your device - No videos are sent externally
  • We don't collect usage statistics - Not even anonymous data is collected

2. Data Processing

The Compressr application:

  • Performs all video compression operations on your device
  • Does not require internet connection
  • Does not send any data to our servers
  • Does not share data with third-party services

3. Data Security

Our security measures:

  • All operations are performed locally
  • Encryption technologies are used
  • No data transfer occurs
  • Regular updates against security vulnerabilities

4. Cookies and Tracking

The Compressr application:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track users
  • Does not use advertising networks
  • Does not use analytics tools

5. Third-Party Services

Our application:

  • Is not integrated with third-party services
  • Does not use external APIs
  • Does not share data

6. Children's Privacy

Compressr does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have accidentally collected data from a child under 13, please contact us.

7. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When important changes occur:

  • We send notifications through the application
  • We update this page
  • We specify the change date

8. Contact

If you have questions about our privacy policy:

  • Email: privacy@compressr.app
  • Support: support@compressr.app

9. Legal Rights

Our users' rights:

  • Right to access data
  • Right to correct data
  • Right to delete data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability

10. Legal Compliance

Compressr is designed to comply with the following legal regulations:

  • KVKK (Turkish Personal Data Protection Law)
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

Note: This privacy policy applies only to the Compressr application. By using our application, you agree to this policy.
